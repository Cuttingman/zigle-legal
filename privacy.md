# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 16, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Zigle ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our content publishing service.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

- **TikTok OAuth tokens**: Temporary access tokens issued by TikTok to authorize content posting on your behalf. These tokens are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
- **Content metadata**: Titles, descriptions, and tags you provide for your videos.

We do NOT collect:
- Personal identification information
- Payment information
- Browsing history
- Any TikTok user data beyond what is required for posting

## 3. How We Use TikTok API Data

Zigle uses the TikTok Content Posting API solely to:
- Post video content to your TikTok profile on your behalf
- Verify that content was successfully uploaded

We do not sell, share, or transfer any TikTok user data to third parties.

## 4. Data Storage

All authentication tokens and content data are stored locally on your own device. Zigle does not operate any cloud servers or databases that store your personal information.

## 5. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are stored only for the duration of your active session. You can revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings at: https://www.tiktok.com/setting

## 6. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with TikTok's API. Your use of TikTok is subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/us/privacy-policy/en

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Revoke Zigle's access to your TikTok account at any time
- Request deletion of any locally stored data

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes.

## 9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: zigle.official@gmail.com
